Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WatchItLater. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together follows:

  • Identity Data: includes username.
  • Contact Data: includes email address.
  • Profile Data: includes your password (securely hashed), your interests, watchlist, watched items, and ratings.
  • Technical Data: internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version.

3. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data for the purpose of operating this educational student project. This includes:

  • Managing your account and providing the requested services.
  • Allowing you to interact with other users (e.g., friend requests).
  • Fetching recommendations and movie data from TMDB on your behalf.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt. However, as this is an educational project, we advise against using sensitive personal data or passwords you use elsewhere.

5. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the TMDB API to fetch movie data. TMDB may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies when their API is accessed. Streaming provider data is provided by JustWatch.

6. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, or restriction of processing. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at watchitlater@software-by.maxigoldmann.de.
